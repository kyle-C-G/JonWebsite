Chocolate Gift Store

August, 2021

Brief

Chocolate Gift Store is an online-based company that sells custom chocolate gifts to people in the UnitedKingdom. Chocolate Gift Store advertises on social media and uses its website as a sales platform. The company's prime business model is to design and produce craft Chocolate gifts that are based on a variety of themes and trends. Easter, Halloween, andChristmas are seasonal sales dominators but the company wants to create advertising campaigns to promote gifts for birthdays and anniversaries.

Example Products

Goal

The Goal is to design a brand image around this Chocolate gift shop that targets a broad demographic which includes a logo, web design and a brand personality.

Process

Because of the D.I.Y and handmade nature of the products, I was inspired to dwell into 1970s punk designs from the likes of Vivienne Westwood and Punk rock fanzines. The "swear chocolate bars" also reflects the edgy and rebellious attitude of punk rock as a whole.
